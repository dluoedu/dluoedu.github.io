Short Bio

I am currently on leave from FIU and don't have access to my FIU email. Please reach out to me: luodongsheng01 [at] gmail [dot] com.

I am an assistant professor at Florida International University. I obtained my doctoral degree at the College of Information Science and Technology, Pennsylvania State University, supervised by Prof. Xiang Zhang in 2022 and my B.Eng degree in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2017.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy AI : Explainable AI, Robust AI, Physical-Aware AI
  • Data/Label Efficient AI: Self-Supervised Learning, Fast Domain Adaptation, Model Compression/ Automatic Feature Selection
  • Multi-Source, Multi-Modality Machine Learning: Federated Learning,Multi-Domain Analysis
  • Data Mining : Time Series, Graph Neural Network, Spatiotemporal Data Analysis

For more details, visit our lab website FIU Trustworthy AI Lab
